Why We Exist?

Didgeridoo is a non-profit organization dedicated to educating the world about the anthropoloigical history of sound, which includes musical instruments, both analog and digital.

Didgeridoo supports people developing relationships with musical instruments. We raise funds to benefit music therapy programs for people with chronic and fatal illnesses, veterans acclimating to life after service, conflict resolution in war zones and we have a vision to introduce music into cultures where music or song was once prohibited.
